My Current Set-up

After work we often come home, throw our stuff in it's place and just lay down and scroll through our phones for hours at most we don't even realize how much hours have past. I was tired of the same thing and wanted to change and start something for myself.

The Aquarium hobby introduced me to the beauties and wonders of little aquatic animals and plants that make a difference in our lives just as the ocean does. It's a wonderful hobby and I would like to share a piece of it with you all.
