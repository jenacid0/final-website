My Recent Aquascape

Working with the enviornment of Aquariums.

This is a special kind of set up for me that I created. The Set up consists of a large black lava rock that we collected when we last visited Tinian. When it comes to scaping your aquarium it's important to understand the you can't just use any kind of aquatic plant nor rock you see at the beach or at home. It's important to study animals and plant life and how they would work together in order to create a safe and home like enovirment for the fish you care for.

Working with Shrimps.

Shrimps was actually one of the first freshwater animals I wanted to work with when starting this aquarium hobby. Other than guppies of course. The thought of breeding and observing these small wonderful creatures and how they live is just so facinating. But they are very tedious and challenging to take care of. Water conditions need to be precise and the right tempurature otherwise the shrimp could have a shock due to the change and die.

I've had my fair share of losing beautiful shrimp and animals due to sudden change in water tempuratures, sad but eventually you start to realize that the same thing happens in real life also anyways especially with Climate change.
